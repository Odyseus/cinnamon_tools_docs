A widget to choose named icons or icon files.

bind_content_widget_entry¶

Since I had the great idea of creating this setting widget with two widgets bound to the setting, I had to come up with this. This attribute is used by the TreeListWidgets.list_edit_factory method to identify IconChooser widget so in addition to call set_property on self.bind_object, it also calls set_text on self.content_widget (a Gtk.Entry) to keep both widgets in sync.
